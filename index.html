About Us

KHAAIYE KHILAAIYE is the name of the stall tag line is yaaden banao (make memories).

Nothing defines the nostalgia of enjoying a perfectly Roasted Corn made with a dash of salt ,spice and lime on a fest evening. It is one of those wonderful food memories that revive the happiness of good old times. Well ,now what you are waiting for come at KHAAIYE KHILAAIYE and have freshly roasted corns to perfection! Corn is a great food choice in winter as it is a nutritional powerhouse ,boasting a rich supply of vitamins,minerals, and fiber. It is also a good source of B-vitamins, such as thiamine and niacin essential minarals like phosphours and magnesium crucial for bone health and overall.

Litti Chokha of Bihar was originated centuries ago as a staple food in the court of Magadha Originating from the state of Bihar, litti chokha is a meal that is popular in different parts of India including Jharkhand and Uttar Pradesh Litti Chokha is believed to have originated in the Magadha region, which includes present-day Bihar and parts of Jharkhand in India. Its roots can be traced back to the ancient times of the Magadha kingdom, which existed from the 6th century BCE to the 5th century CE. However, the precise origin story of Litti Chokha may not have been explicitly documented and might have evolved over time through cultural influences and culinary practices.
